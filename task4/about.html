Who We Are

Google is a global technology company focused on improving the ways people connect with information. From search and advertising to cloud computing and artificial intelligence, we strive to make information universally accessible and useful.

Our Mission

Our mission is to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful. We believe in using technology to make people’s lives easier and empower individuals everywhere.

Our Products

We build products used by billions of people, including Search, Maps, Gmail, Android, Chrome, and YouTube. We’re constantly innovating to improve and expand our offerings in AI, cloud computing, and more.

Join Us

We’re always looking for curious and creative people to join our team. Whether you're a coder, designer, researcher, or strategist, there's a place for you at Google.
